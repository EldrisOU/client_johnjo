Fonterra x Linde MH

Brand & Corporate

This project was a partnership announcement film for Fonterra's Darfield processing site. The challenge was to tell a corporate B2B story in a way that felt human and authentic.

We focused on real people talking about a real business relationship—interviews with key stakeholders combined with dynamic footage of Fonterra logistics operations and Linde material handling equipment in action.
