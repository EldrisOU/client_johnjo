TDX — Brand Film

Brand & Corporate

TDX are New Zealand's leading specialists in transformer and distribution equipment. They needed a brand piece that went beyond the hardware to show the expertise and scale of their operation.

We spent three days filming across multiple sites — from heavy workshops to active substations and field service operations. The goal was to capture the grit and precision of their work, highlighting the people who keep the power on.

"The finished product clearly reflects what we want to put into the marketplace."

— Client Feedback
