Common Questions

How much does a video cost?

It depends entirely on scope. A simple interview-based piece might be $3,000–$5,000. A full brand film with multiple shoot days, aerial work, and motion graphics could be $15,000–$30,000+. I'll always give you a clear quote before we start.

How long does a project take?

Most projects run 4–8 weeks from brief to delivery. Tight timelines are possible for the right projects — just let me know your constraints upfront.

Do you do same-day edits for events?

Yes, for the right events. It requires specific planning, so talk to me early if that's what you need.

Can you work with our internal team?

Absolutely. I'm happy to collaborate with your marketing people, provide footage for them to edit, or work alongside other creatives. Whatever setup works best for your organisation.

What if we're not sure what we need?

That's fine — it's often where the best projects start. Book a call and we'll figure it out together.
