14+ Years Experience • CAA Part 101 Certified • Based in Otepoti Dunedin

I'm a Dunedin-based filmmaker helping New Zealand businesses tell stronger stories.

I work across the South Island and beyond — from heavy industry and construction to science, technology, councils, and adventure tourism. If you've got people doing interesting work, I want to help you show it.

I didn't set out to be a filmmaker. I came to New Zealand in 2004 from the north of England, chasing snow and adventure. Ended up as a ski instructor in Wanaka, then started playing with cameras, making little edits of mates snowboarding. Those edits turned into paid gigs. The paid gigs turned into a business.

After a decade building Flashworks Media — a B-Corp certified video production company — I wanted something different. Less managing, more making. Less chasing scale, more focusing on craft. So I shifted my life to Dunedin, simplified the business, and got back to what I love: working directly with clients, shooting beautiful footage, and telling stories that matter.

My Mission

How I Work
